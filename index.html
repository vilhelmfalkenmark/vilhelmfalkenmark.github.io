About Vilhelm Falkenmark

Random stuff

My name is Vilhelm Falkenmark and I am currently 28 years old

I was born In Stockholm, Sweden on the 22nd of june 1986 as the second-eldest sibling among four.

When I was a kid I was obsessed with Lego, Turtles and Batman. When I turned 10 I discovered Kiss (the band) and my life changed forever to come. I started playing guitar when I was 13 and it has been my main hobby since then. During my youth I spent my free-time playing in various bands and music projects.

After I finished high-school I went to Australia for 6 months with one of my best friends to "discover myself" and "find out what I really want to do with my life". I did not really achieve that. Instead I applied for an education in Business and Economics. I moved to Uppsala when I turned 20 and studied there for 4 years and 6 months abroad in Maastricht, The Netherlands. I took my Degree of Masters in Business and Economics in January 2011.

After my graduation I started working for a small E-commerce as a web-administator and in their customer-service. After 1.5 years I grew restless and wanted to do something else (as many in generation Y do). I started working for another company in the corporate group as a content manager. This was my first contact with "programming". If you can call extremely basic HTML-coding "programming". I worked there for another 1.5 years until I decided to work part-time and study a course in basic programming in my spare-time.

After I "graduated" the course I was sure what I wanted to be when I grew up, A feeling I unfortunately never felt during my time in Uppsala. I applied in the last minute for the 2-year long education in Front-End Development and got in.

Education

  • 3-year High-School Degree from Norra Reals Gymnasium. Graduation 2005.
  • 4.5 year Degree of Masters in Business and Economics from Uppsala University. Graduation 2011.
  • I am Currently Attending Nackademins Yrkeshögskola in the 2-year long education in Front-End Development. Graduation in june 2016.

Skills

  • Strong Skills in

    • HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript (Front-End Programming). I work or try to catch with all the modern techiques and framworks such as Node.js, Angular.js, Sass, jQuery & Bootstrap to name a few.
    • Various Graphical software such as Adobe Photoshop, After Effects and Illustrator
    • CMS-Systems, Wordpress and Joomla
    • Microsoft Office Package, Especially Excel.
    • E-Commerce

  • Experience in

    • PHP, Ruby and C#
    • Search Engine Optimization
    • Various Business software, Mamut Visma, Specter, Askås, Fortnox.

Previous Employment

  • Retail and Brands Logistics during the summers of 2005, 2006, 2007 and 2008.
  • EF language courses as a course leader on Malta during the summer of 2009.

  • Internet Retail Holding
